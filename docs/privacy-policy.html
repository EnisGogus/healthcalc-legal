Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2024

Summary: HealthCalc respects your privacy. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal health data.

1. Information We Collect

HealthCalc is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we collect:

  • Health Calculations: All your health calculations (BMI, BMR, TDEE, etc.) are stored locally on your device only. We never upload this data to any server.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports. This helps us fix bugs and improve the app. No personal health data is included in crash reports.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your health calculation results
  • Your location
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. Data Storage

All your health data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. If you uninstall the app, all local data will be deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: For anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability. Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

HealthCalc is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
